# SDM Engagement Engine — Daily Workflow Guide
### Free, Internationally Accessible Tools Only

This guide turns the Field Manual's steps into a repeatable daily routine. Every tool listed has a free tier and works internationally with just an internet connection.

---

## Daily Workflow (≈45–60 minutes/day)

### 🌅 Morning Block (15–20 min) — Outreach & Initiation
1. **Open Google Sheets CRM** → Identify 5–10 new prospects to contact today (Stage = "Initial Outreach" or blank).
2. **Send eBook outreach**:
   - Email via **Gmail** (use the phone script as your email template).
   - Or message via **WhatsApp** for a more personal touch.
3. **Log it immediately** in Google Sheets: Date_Contacted, Outreach_Method, Stage.
4. **Set a Google Calendar reminder** for 1 week out (Follow_Up_Date) — don't follow up before the download is confirmed.

### ☀️ Midday Block (10–15 min) — Tracking & Confirmation
1. **Check Bitly link clicks** (free analytics) to see who clicked your eBook link.
2. Update **Download_Status** and **Download_Date** in your CRM for anyone who engaged.
3. For non-responders past 2–3 days: send a polite **WhatsApp** check-in text.

### 🌤️ Afternoon Block (10 min) — Community Engagement
1. Spend 10 minutes on **Facebook** (teacher/school group) and **Instagram** (family group):
   - Comment, like, repost — no selling.
   - Tag prospects with their unique `#NameSDMPro` hashtag (track this in the Social_Tag column).
2. On **LinkedIn**: repost one official Signal Daddy Media post, like/comment on 2–3 SDM Pro posts.

### 🌙 Evening Block (10–15 min) — Follow-Up Calls & Referrals
1. Check Google Calendar for today's scheduled **Follow_Up_Date** entries.
2. Make warm calls via **WhatsApp** or regular phone:
   - Use the family-focused script: *"How did your child enjoy the story/recipe?"*
3. Ask satisfied contacts for:
   - An Amazon review
   - A referral (who else might enjoy this?)
   - A company introduction (CEP/CSR angle)
4. Update **Family_Feedback**, **Referral_Source**, and **Stage** columns.

## Weekly Tasks (Set a recurring Google Calendar event)

| Day | Task |
|---|---|
| **Monday** | Plan week's outreach list (10–15 new prospects) in Google Sheets |
| **Wednesday** | Post a "Recipe Challenge" video/photo on Instagram |
| **Friday** | Review analytics (Bitly clicks, CRM stage distribution) — per the manual's "check Friday" best practice |
| **Friday** | Send 2 LinkedIn reposts to stay qualified for the Quarterly Repost Challenge |

## Simple Daily Checklist (Copy into Google Keep or Google Docs)

- [ ] Contact 5–10 new prospects (email/WhatsApp)
- [ ] Log all contacts in CRM (Google Sheets)
- [ ] Check link clicks (Bitly) and update download status
- [ ] Engage 10 min on Facebook + Instagram (no selling)
- [ ] Repost/like on LinkedIn
- [ ] Make scheduled follow-up calls (1 week post-download)
- [ ] Ask for reviews/referrals from engaged contacts
- [ ] Update CRM stages before logging off

## Notes on International Use
- All tools above run in-browser or via free mobile apps — no purchase required, no region lock for core features.
- WhatsApp is preferred over SMS for international outreach since it's free over internet and widely used.
- Google Workspace free tools (Sheets, Docs, Calendar, Gmail) sync across devices, so this workflow works whether you're on desktop or phone.
