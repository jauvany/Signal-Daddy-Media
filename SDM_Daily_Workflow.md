# SDM Engagement Engine — Daily Execution Workflow

A repeatable daily rhythm for running the SDM Pro Engagement Pathway. Pair this with the CRM tracker so every action gets logged.

---

## Morning Block (15–20 min): Review & Plan

1. **Check the CRM Dashboard**
   - Pull yesterday's download/QR scan activity.
   - Flag anyone who downloaded **7+ days ago** — they're ready for a Warm Call.
2. **Sort today's contacts into 3 buckets:**
   - 🆕 New prospects to start (Step 1: Initial Outreach)
   - ⏳ Pending downloads to nudge (Step 2: Confirmation & Tracking)
   - ✅ Confirmed downloads ready for Family Follow-Up (Step 3)
3. **Set your daily targets:**
   - New outreach: 5–10 contacts
   - Warm calls (post-download): 3–5 contacts
   - Social touchpoints: 2 posts/comments minimum

---

## Block 1: New Outreach (Step 1)

For each new prospect/peer/organization contact:
- [ ] Call or email using the Initial Outreach script (book gift framing — never sales framing).
- [ ] Log in CRM: `Date_Contacted`, `Outreach_Method`, `Book_Sent_Date`.
- [ ] Set `Stage = Initial Outreach`.

---

## Block 2: Confirmation & Nudges (Step 2)

For contacts who haven't downloaded yet:
- [ ] Send a polite check-in (email or text) if no activity after a few days.
- [ ] Text the download link directly if you have their number.
- [ ] Update `Download_Status` in CRM.
- **Rule:** Do NOT call again until download is confirmed — let the tracking data trigger your next move.

---

## Block 3: Warm Calls — Family Follow-Up (Step 3)

For contacts who downloaded ~1 week ago:
- [ ] Call with a family-oriented opener: *"How did your child enjoy the story?"*
- [ ] Log `Family_Feedback` in CRM.
- [ ] Gently prompt: Amazon review + join the Instagram/Facebook community.
- [ ] Update `Stage = Family Follow-Up`.

---

## Block 4: Corporate / Referral Pitch (Step 4–5, as relevant)

Only for warmed contacts who've had a positive family conversation:
- [ ] Ask: *"Who would be the best person at your company to talk to about a literacy program like this?"*
- [ ] Ask for referrals to clubs, lodges, churches, schools, or their employer's HR/CSR contact.
- [ ] Log `Referral_Source` and update `Stage = Corporate Pitch` or `Referral`.

---

## Block 5: Social & Community Engagement (Daily, 10–15 min)

- [ ] Post or repost 1–2 pieces of Signal Daddy Media content on LinkedIn.
- [ ] Comment/like on other SDM Pros' posts (boosts algorithm visibility).
- [ ] Tag any newly engaged prospects with your personal `#YourNameSDMPro` tag in the community pages (no selling in-feed).
- [ ] Check Instagram/Facebook groups for family activity to engage with.

---

## End-of-Day Wrap-Up (10 min)

- [ ] Update CRM `Next_Action` field for every contact touched today.
- [ ] Review the week's repost count (need 2+/week for the Quarterly Repost Challenge).
- [ ] Note any prospect who's gone quiet 10+ days — schedule a final nudge or move to a "Dormant" stage.

---

## Weekly Checkpoints (set a recurring reminder)

| Day | Task |
|---|---|
| **Monday** | Plan the week's outreach list; review weekend social activity |
| **Wednesday** | Mid-week CRM cleanup — confirm download/follow-up dates are accurate |
| **Friday** | Pull analytics report (downloads, QR scans, referrals); check repost challenge progress |

---

## Quick Reference: Stage Definitions (match your CRM)

1. **Initial Outreach** — first contact made, book sent
2. **Tracking** — waiting on download confirmation
3. **Family Follow-Up** — download confirmed, warm call done
4. **Corporate Pitch** — introduced CEP/CSR angle, seeking internal contact
5. **Referral** — secured an introduction or referral lead
6. **Conversion** — review left, joined community, business meeting scheduled
7. **Dormant** — no engagement after multiple touchpoints

---

*Tip: Keep your daily targets small and consistent (5–10 new touches/day) rather than batching — the trackable, time-spaced nature of this workflow depends on natural pacing, not bulk blasts.*
